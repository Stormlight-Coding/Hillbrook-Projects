Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion at Hillbrook

The twenty-first century is defined by a growing global society that challenges intolerance and values differences as a way to enrich and deepen our world. At Hillbrook, cultural competency is the willingness to see oneself situated in a complex and diverse world, to seek understanding and appreciation of difference, to develop empathy, and to challenge biases and discrimination.
